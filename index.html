Hello!

I'm a software engineer at Google. I'm interested in natural language processing, machine learning, and software engineering.

Outside of computer science, I enjoy playing violin and tennis.

Previously, I...

  • studied computer science at the University of Washington as an M.S. student in the Allen School.
  • was a researcher in Noah's Ark and UW NLP where I was advised by Noah Smith.
  • worked at Unity Technologies on the Unity Machine Learning Agents Toolkit.
  • worked at NVIDIA on training autonomous robots in simulation.
  • was a researcher in UW PLSE where I was advised by Michael Ernst.
  • interned at Amazon on the Alexa Machine Learning team.
  • studied at ETH Zürich as a computer science exchange student.
