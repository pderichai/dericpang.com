Hello!

I'm a software engineer at Google. I'm interested in natural language processing, machine learning, and software engineering.

Outside of computer science, I enjoy playing violin and tennis.

Previously...

  • I was an M.S. student studying computer science in the Allen School at the University of Washington.
  • I was a researcher in Noah's Ark and UW NLP where I was advised by Noah Smith.
  • I was a machine learning intern at Unity Technologies working on the Unity Machine Learning Agents Toolkit.
  • I was an applied research intern at NVIDIA where I worked on training autonomous robots in simulation.
  • I was a researcher in the Allen School's Programming Languages & Software Engineering group where I was advised by Michael Ernst.
  • I was an intern in Alexa Machine Learning at Amazon where I worked on automatically converting production neural networks between different deep learning frameworks.
  • I studied at ETH Zürich as a computer science exchange student.
